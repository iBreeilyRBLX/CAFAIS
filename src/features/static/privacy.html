Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 6, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use our Discord bot verification system, we collect:

  • Discord Information: Your Discord user ID, username, and server nickname
  • Roblox Information: Your Roblox user ID, username, and display name (obtained via OAuth)
  • Activity Data: Event attendance, points earned, and command usage

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Link your Discord account to your Roblox profile for verification
  • Update your Discord server nickname with your Roblox display name
  • Track faction activity, points, and event participation
  • Manage roles, ranks, and permissions within our Discord server

3. Data Storage

All data is stored securely in our private PostgreSQL database. We do not share your information with third parties except as required for bot functionality (Discord API, Roblox OAuth).

4. OAuth & Third-Party Services

We use Roblox OAuth for verification. When you authorize our app, Roblox provides us with your public profile information. We do not store OAuth tokens; they are used only during the verification process.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your data (use /unverify command)
  • View your stored information (use /verify-status command)
  • Opt-out of verification at any time

6. Data Retention

We retain your verification data as long as you remain in the Discord server. If you leave or use the /unverify command, your verification data is removed.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including CSRF protection, encrypted connections (HTTPS), and secure database access controls.

8. Contact

For privacy concerns or data requests, contact us via our Discord server.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of any changes.
